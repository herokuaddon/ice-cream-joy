Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

IceCreamJoy (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to handling your information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when using our website. By accessing or using IceCreamJoy, you agree to this policy.

We may automatically collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, referral source, and approximate geographic location derived from IP address. This information helps us improve performance, content relevance, and user experience.

We may also collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address, for example when contacting us or subscribing to updates. We only collect personal information that you choose to submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website.
  • Respond to inquiries, feedback, or support requests.
  • Analyze content performance and user behavior trends.
  • Enhance security and prevent spam or misuse.
  • Support advertising and analytics functions.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

IceCreamJoy uses cookies and similar technologies to improve browsing experience, remember preferences, and understand visitor behavior. Cookies are small text files stored by your browser.

Some cookies are essential for website functionality, while others are used for analytics and advertising. You can manage or disable cookies through browser settings, though some features may not work properly afterward.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising Partners

We may display ads through Google AdSense and other third-party advertising providers. These vendors may use cookies (including Google’s DoubleClick cookie) to serve relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can manage ad personalization using Google Ads Settings. You may also review additional opt-out tools through resources such as www.aboutads.info.

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to collect aggregate usage information like page views, session duration, bounce rate, and referral channels. This data helps us optimize website structure and publish more useful recipe and dessert content.

Our website may contain links to third-party sites, products, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before sharing information.

7. Data Security

8. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for legitimate business, legal, or operational purposes. Non-personal aggregate analytics data may be retained for trend analysis and performance reporting.

9. Children’s Privacy

IceCreamJoy is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child submitted personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may submit such requests using the contact details below, and we will respond as required by applicable law.
